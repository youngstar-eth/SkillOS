## How to use this file

1. **Copy the template block** (between the `---` rules) into your mail / DM client.
2. **Replace every `[ALL_CAPS_PLACEHOLDER]`** before send. Do not leave any
   placeholder unfilled — empty `[FOO]` in a sent email is a credibility hit
   larger than skipping the send.
3. **Voice anchor:** founder-voice (Inanç), conviction-era market discipline.
   Tone reference: Papa Dari "Crypto VC in 2026" (April 16 2026, "real
   fundamentals" thread) + Stacy Muur active-deployer filter.
4. **Forbidden phrases (per `communication-frame-v2.md` §9):**
   `substrate intelligence` · `foundation model training` · `AI oracle` ·
   `token launch` · `governance token` · `$SP token` · `Anthropic partner` ·
   `buyback` · specific multi-product vertical timelines · specific
   next-billion-user metrics. If any draft contains one of these, **rewrite
   before sending.**
5. **Forbidden form:** no hashtags, no engagement bait, no emoji clusters,
   no link-only DMs (always one sentence of substance + link).

## Appendix A — Pre-send checklist

Before sending any of the four templates:

- [ ] No placeholder still in `[BRACKETS]`.
- [ ] No forbidden phrase from `communication-frame-v2.md` §9.
- [ ] Demo URL loads cleanly in incognito and shows a tx hash within 30
      seconds of arrival (don't link a broken demo — single biggest
      credibility hit in this market).
- [ ] If `[RECENT_DEAL_REFERENCE]` or `[SPECIFIC_THESIS_HOOK]` was inferred
      from social proof and not their actual portfolio / writing, replace or
      drop. Fabricated thesis-mirror is the fastest blocklist trigger.
- [ ] If you reference Skillz/Papaya, the dollar figure is **$420M** and the
      vehicle is the **Lanham Act verdict, April 2026**. Do not paraphrase
      the legal vehicle — paraphrasing reads as bluff.
- [ ] Subject line for Template 1 stays verbatim — the "no token playbook"
      clause is the conviction-era signal.

## Appendix B — When not to send these templates

- Do **not** send Template 1 / 2 to anyone who has already replied "not a
  fit" within the last 12 months. Use a categorically new vehicle (warm
  intro from a portfolio founder, conference IRL, mutual press mention) or
  wait for Phase 2 mainnet to re-open the conversation.
- Do **not** send Template 4 to a recipient who replied with any signal,
  even a soft "interesting, not now" — those go in a separate nurture
  cadence (not in scope for this file).
- Do **not** use Template 2A or 2B to cold-DM anyone who has explicitly
  said "email is better" — switch to Template 1 even if the original
  thread started on X.
